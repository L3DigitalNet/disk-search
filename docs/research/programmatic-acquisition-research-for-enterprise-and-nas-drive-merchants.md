## Bottom line

For your specific use case, the best near-term additions are **ServerPartDeals**, **goHardDrive**, **WD Recertified**, **Seagate Recertified**, and **B&H**. ServerPartDeals is the cleanest value-target expansion: its public product pages expose exact price, stock count, condition, and SKU on a Shopify storefront. goHardDrive is also attractive because it appears to run a very old-school HTML/ASP storefront with public product pages that search engines can read directly, including price and stock snippets. WD and Seagate matter because they are first-party recertified channels, but both official stores are more JS-heavy and less convenient than ServerPartDeals or goHardDrive. B&H is not a recertifier, but it is one of the easiest mainstream retailers to ingest because its pages are highly crawlable and it has an official affiliate program.
